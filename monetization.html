🧭 How to Plan Your Trip

  • Stay central for your first visit to save travel time
  • Most attractions are walkable in the city center
  • Local bakeries are the cheapest and easiest food option
  • Evening walks along the river are a local favorite

📍 Best Areas to Stay in Belgrade

  • Stari Grad — central, historic, walkable
  • Dorćol — cafés, relaxed local atmosphere
  • Zemun — riverside views, quieter area
  • Vračar — residential, cafés and restaurants
Tip: Booking early and comparing different areas can significantly reduce your travel costs in Belgrade.
