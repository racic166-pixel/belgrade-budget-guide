Practical Planning Tips

  • Book accommodation near public transport
  • Walk central areas when possible
  • Use local bakeries for affordable breakfasts
  • Visit riverside areas during sunset hours
  • Carry some cash for smaller cafés and bakeries

Useful Areas to Stay In

  • Stari Grad — central and walkable
  • Dorćol — cafés and quieter atmosphere
  • Zemun — riverside and more relaxed
  • Vračar — residential with restaurants and cafés
