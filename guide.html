How to Spend 3 Days in Belgrade on a Budget

Belgrade is one of Europe’s cheapest capitals. This guide shows you exactly how to explore it efficiently without overspending.

Day 1 — City Center

Knez Mihailova → Kalemegdan Fortress → Skadarlija nightlife

Day 2 — Local Areas

Zemun riverside → cafés → river clubs (splavovi)

Day 3 — Relax Day

Ada Ciganlija → street food → Dorćol cafés

Budget

€30–€50 per day depending on lifestyle

Final Tip

Walk everywhere, eat local bakeries, avoid tourist traps — that’s how you travel Belgrade cheaply.
