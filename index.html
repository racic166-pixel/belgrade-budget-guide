Plan Your Trip to Belgrade

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most underrated travel destinations. It combines low prices, strong nightlife, historic landmarks, river views, and a growing café culture. It is ideal for budget travelers and short city breaks.

Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade offers a unique mix of history, nightlife, culture, and affordability. It is one of the best budget-friendly cities in Europe for first-time travelers.

🏙️ Best Places

Kalemegdan Fortress

Historic fortress with panoramic river views.

Knez Mihailova

Main pedestrian street full of life.

🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala
Budget tip: bakeries are cheapest + most authentic

💰 Budget Tips

• €25–€50/day is enough for comfortable travel
• Public transport is very cheap
• Walking is the best way to explore
• Stay slightly outside city center for better prices

🧭 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Kalemegdan → Knez Mihailova → Skadarlija

Day 2: Museums → Dorćol cafés → nightlife

Day 3: Ada Ciganlija → riverside walk → sunset spots
