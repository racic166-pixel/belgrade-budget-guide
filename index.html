Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most underrated cities — affordable, energetic, full of history, nightlife, and riverside views. Perfect for budget travelers.

🏙️ Best Places

🍽️ Food

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala
Budget tip: bakeries are cheapest option

💰 Budget Tips

• €25–€50/day is enough
• Use public transport (very cheap)
• Walk everywhere (city is compact)
• Stay outside tourist center for cheaper prices
