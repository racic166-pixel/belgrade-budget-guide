Belgrade Travel Guide Overview

Belgrade budget travel: This guide helps you explore the best places, food spots, cafés, and attractions while keeping your trip affordable and efficient.

Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most underrated cities. It offers cheap travel costs, strong nightlife, historic landmarks, and riverside culture.

🏙️ Top Places in Belgrade

Kalemegdan Fortress

Historic fortress with river views and sunset spots.

Knez Mihailova Street

Main pedestrian zone filled with shops and cafés.

🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala

💰 Budget Strategy

• €25–€50 per day travel budget
• Use public transport (cheap + reliable)
• Walk whenever possible
• Avoid tourist-heavy hotel zones
