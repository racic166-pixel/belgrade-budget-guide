Best Places to Visit in Belgrade

Knez Mihailova

Main pedestrian street with cafés, shops, and historic architecture.

Kalemegdan Fortress

Historic fortress with river views, parks, and sunset spots.

Skadarlija

Old bohemian street with traditional restaurants and nightlife.

Food & Cafés in Belgrade

Belgrade offers affordable food compared to most European capitals, with strong café culture and local restaurants everywhere.

  • Traditional bakeries (burek, pastries, fast cheap meals)
  • Riverside cafés in Zemun
  • Modern coffee shops in Dorćol
  • Local grill restaurants outside tourist zones

Budget Travel Tips

Use Public Transport

Buses and trams are the cheapest way to move around the city.

Stay in Local Areas

Dorćol, Vračar, and Zemun often offer better value than tourist center hotels.

Walk the City Center

Most attractions are close together — walking saves money and time.
