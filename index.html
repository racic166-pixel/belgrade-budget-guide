🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Cheap Local Food

Bakeries (burek), pljeskavica, fast and cheap meals everywhere.

Traditional Food

Serbian grilled meat, stews and local specialties.

💰 Budget Tips

  • Daily budget: €25–€50
  • Eat at bakeries instead of restaurants
  • Use public transport (very cheap)
  • Walk — city is compact
  • Stay in Dorćol, Vračar or Zemun
