Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most affordable and underrated cities. It combines nightlife, history, river views, and strong local food culture.

🏙️ Best Places in Belgrade

Kalemegdan Fortress

Historic landmark with river views and sunset spots.

Knez Mihailova Street

Main pedestrian zone full of cafés and shops.

🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala

💰 Budget Travel Tips

• €25–€50 per day average budget
• Walk instead of taxis
• Use public transport (very cheap)
• Stay outside tourist center for savings

Day 2: Museums → Dorćol cafés → nightlife

Day 3: Ada Ciganlija → riverside walk → sunset spots
