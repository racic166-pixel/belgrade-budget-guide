🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Cheap and local options: bakeries (burek), street grills, fast meals.

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, sarma, kajmak.

Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala.

💰 Budget Tips

  • €25–€50/day is enough for comfortable travel
  • Use public transport (cheap buses/trams)
  • Eat at bakeries instead of restaurants
  • Walk — city is compact
  • Stay in Dorćol, Vračar or Zemun
