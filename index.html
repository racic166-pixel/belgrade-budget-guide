Plan Your Trip to Belgrade

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most underrated travel destinations. It offers affordable prices, strong nightlife, historic landmarks, riverside areas, and a growing café culture. Most travelers can explore the city comfortably on a budget of €25–€50 per day.

Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade combines history, nightlife, rivers, and culture in one compact and walkable city. It is especially popular among budget travelers and solo explorers.

🏙️ Best Places

Kalemegdan Fortress

Historic fortress with river views and sunset spots.

Knez Mihailova

Main pedestrian street full of life and cafés.

🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala
Budget tip: bakeries are cheapest + most authentic option

💰 Budget Tips

• €25–€50/day is enough for comfortable travel
• Public transport is cheap and reliable
• Walking saves money + gives best experience
• Stay slightly outside city center for lower prices

🧭 3-Day Belgrade Itinerary

Day 1: Kalemegdan → Knez Mihailova → Skadarlija

Day 2: Museums + Dorćol cafés + nightlife

Day 3: Ada Ciganlija + riverside walk + sunset spots
