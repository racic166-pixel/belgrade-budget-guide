Why Visit Belgrade?

Belgrade is one of Europe’s most underrated cities — cheap, energetic, full of history, nightlife, and riverside views. Perfect for budget travelers and city explorers.

🍽️ Food in Belgrade

Must try: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma
Best areas: Dorćol, Zemun, Savamala
Budget tip: bakeries are the cheapest + best option

💰 Budget Tips

• €25–€50 per day is enough
• Use public transport (very cheap)
• Walk instead of taxis
• Stay outside strict city center for cheaper prices

💡 Pro Travel Tips

✔ Walk from Kalemegdan to Skadarlija at sunset (best free experience)
✔ Try local bakeries instead of restaurants for cheap food
✔ Stay in Dorćol for best balance of price + location
✔ Evenings = best vibe in riverside areas
