About Belgrade

Belgrade is the capital of Serbia and one of Europe’s most energetic cities. It combines history, brutalist architecture, river life, nightlife, and strong café culture. The city is split between old Austro-Hungarian influence and modern urban districts, giving it a very unique identity.

🏙️ Places to Visit

Kalemegdan Fortress

Massive historic fortress overlooking the Danube and Sava rivers. Best visited at sunset for panoramic views and photos.

Knez Mihailova Street

🍽️ Food & Cafés

Belgrade food culture is heavily based on grilled meat, pastries, and traditional Balkan dishes.

Must try foods: ćevapi, pljeskavica, burek, sarma, kajmak

Best areas for food: Dorćol (modern cafés), Skadarlija (traditional restaurants), Zemun (riverside dining)

Day 2: Museums → Dorćol cafés → New Belgrade architecture → nightlife

Day 3: Ada Ciganlija (lake area) → riverside walk → sunset views over bridges
